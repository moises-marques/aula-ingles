English Introduction

Hello, and welcome! This material was created to help beginners start learning English in a simple and practical way. Here, you will learn essential vocabulary, useful expressions, pronunciation, and basic grammar — all explained in a friendly and easy-to-understand style. Whether you're starting from zero or already know a little, this guide will help you take your first steps with confidence. Let’s study together, take it slow, and make English a natural part of your daily life.
